Asian Ground Beef Noodle Bowl

An asian beef bowl.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • green onions
  • water
  • ramen
  • asian veggies
  • hoisin sauce
  • sriracha
  • brown sugar
  • ginger

Instructions

  1. Cook beef and onions
  2. Add water, ramen, and veggies
  3. Combine the rest of ingredients in a bowl
  4. Put everything back into the pot and combine
