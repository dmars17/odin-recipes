Grilled Steak

Steak on the grill

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • salt
  • oil
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Let steaks rest for 20 min
  2. Heat grill to high
  3. Brush steaks with oil
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  5. place steaks on grill
  6. turn after 5 min
  7. cook to internal temp of 140
  8. remove from grill
  9. let sit for 5 min
